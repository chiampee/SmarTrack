Quick Start Guide

1

📌 Pin the Extension

Make the extension easy to access by pinning it to your toolbar:

📍 How to Pin:

  1. Look for the puzzle piece icon 🧩 in the top-right corner of Chrome
  2. Click it to open the extensions menu
  3. Find "Smart Research Tracker" in the list
  4. Click the 📌 pin icon next to it
  5. Done! The extension icon will now be visible in your toolbar ✨
2

💾 Save Your First Link

Visit any webpage you want to save, then click the extension icon in your toolbar. Add a title, label, and priority, then click "Save to Research".

💡 Pro tip: Use keyboard shortcut Ctrl+Shift+R (or Cmd+Shift+R on Mac) for quick access!

3

📊 View Your Dashboard

Click "Open Dashboard" in the extension popup to see all your saved links organized beautifully with powerful search, filters, and AI features.
